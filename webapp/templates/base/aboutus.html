About Us
Finaskus Technologies Pvt Ltd ("FinAskus") is here to simplify smart investing. We combine the latest technology with time-tested investing principles, making it a convenient and powerful way for you to invest for your financial goals with the added convenience of no paperwork. Through diversification, automated intelligent rebalancing, and better guided behavior, FinAskus customers can expect higher returns than a typical DIY (Do-It-Yourself) investor.

Your investments are safe with FinAskus. FinAskus portfolios are completely liquid, and you can access your money at any time.
